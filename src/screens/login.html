About us

Who we are?

Welcome to the B.Tech Project Management System, an innovative platform designed to streamline the management of final year projects in engineering institutions. Our mission is to enhance communication, ensure timely completion, and support students and mentors through features such as project tracking, meeting scheduling, assignment submission, machine learning-based completion predictions, and AI-assisted tools. By addressing inefficiencies and fostering collaboration, we aim to provide a user-friendly, efficient solution for managing and completing academic projects.

Motive

What we do?

At the B.Tech Project Management System, we provide a comprehensive platform that simplifies and optimizes the management of final year projects for engineering students and mentors. Our system offers a suite of tools, including a centralized project tracking dashboard, meeting scheduler, assignment submission portal, and communication features. We leverage machine learning to predict project completion timelines. Our goal is to facilitate efficient project management, improve collaboration, and ensure successful project completion through a user-friendly and robust digital environment.
